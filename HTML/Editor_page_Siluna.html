My Contributions

Home Page, Navigation Bar, and Footer

"TourUP," a travel website promoting sustainable tourism. The page features a navigation bar with links to various sections, including Home, Gallery, Services, Content, SiteMap, About Us, and Feedback, along with icons for cart, wishlist, and profile. The main content includes a hero section with a quote and description of sustainable tourism, a showcase of top destinations in Sri Lanka with detailed cards, a welcoming message from TourUP, and a variety of accommodation options. The footer includes social media links, contact information, and the company logo, ensuring a comprehensive and user-friendly experience.
