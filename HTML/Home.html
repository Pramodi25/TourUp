DAMBULLA

"Ancient Wonders"

  • Dambulla is home to the
    iconic Golden Temple,
    featuring ancient cave temples
    adorned with Buddha statues
    and murals. The nearby Sigiriya Rock Fortress
    and Pidurangala Rock
    provide historical intrigue.

COLOMBO

"Urban Vibrance"

  • The bustling capital,
    blends modern attractions
    with historical sites like
    the Gangaramaya Temple and
    the National Museum.
    Its lively markets,
    beachfront promenade
    at Galle Face Green,
    and vibrant dining scene
    make it a dynamic city
    to explore.
